SAINIK SCHOOL

School Admission is confined to boys in VI & IX. Admissions are granted on the basis of the merit as per written examination, interview and medical.

RIMC

The Rashtriya Indian Military College provides public school education to young boys specially selected through an All India competitive examination.

NDA/NA

The NDA Selection Process is divided into two stages, the first is a written examination and the second is SSB Interview.

CDS

The CDS Selection Process is divided into two stages, the first is a written examination and the second is SSB Interview..

AFCAT

The Air Force Common Admission Test is conducted by the Indian Air Force twice every year for selection of Officers in various branches.

AC CAPF

Central Police Forces (Assistant Commandants) Examination conducts by UPSC for different central forces in India namely SSB, BSF, ITBP, CISF, CRPF.

JNU ENTRANCE

Jawaharlal Nehru University conducts its Entrance Examination annually for UG and PG courses. The exam is also known as JNU CEEB.

PSYCHOLOGY NET
